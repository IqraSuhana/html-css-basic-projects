Dr. Abdul Qadeer Khan

1936 - 2021

Father of Nuclear program in Pakistan

Abdul Qadeer (AQ) Khan is responsible for Pakistan acquiring the technology to build an atomic bomb. He was born in 1936 in British India, moved to the newly-created country of Pakistan in 1952, and then moved to Europe in 1961 to pursue a degree in metallurgical engineering. He is widely regarded as the founder of gas-centrifuge enrichment technology for Pakistan’s nuclear deterrent program. Pakistan’s nuclear weapons program is a source of extreme national pride. As its “father”, A.Q. Khan, who headed Pakistan’s nuclear program for some 25 years, is considered a national hero.
